MediTrack Diabetes

Stay on top of prescriptions, supplies, and medication reminders – anywhere, anytime.

About the App

MediTrack Diabetes is a personal health companion built to help people living with diabetes manage their prescriptions, supplies, and reminders with confidence.

Key Features

  • Prescription Tracking – Add medications or supplies and log frequency & dosage.
  • Inventory Management – Monitor stock levels, lot numbers, and expiration dates.
  • Reminders – Get timely push notifications to stay on schedule.
  • Reports – Generate adherence summaries and export to share with providers.
  • Emergency QR – Share critical medical details with first responders.
