MediTrack Diabetes – Age Suitability

Last updated:

Intended Audience

MediTrack Diabetes is designed for general audiences, including adults and teens with parental guidance. The app helps users track prescriptions, supplies, and medication reminders. It does not contain user-generated public content, gambling, violence, or sexual content.

Children & Parental Guidance

  • The app is not intended for children under 13 without parental/guardian supervision.
  • For users under the age of consent in their region, a parent/guardian should manage the account and data.
  • MediTrack Diabetes does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Content & Features

  • No mature themes, shocking content, or simulated gambling.
  • Notifications are limited to medication/supply reminders and low-stock alerts.
  • No public social features or in-app chat/forums.

Data Handling (Summary)

  • We collect basic account information and prescription/supply data the user enters to provide core functionality.
  • Data is processed securely and is not shared with third parties for advertising.
  • Parents/guardians may request deletion of a minor’s account data via the contact below.
